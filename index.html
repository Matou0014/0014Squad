Founded by two schoolmates in September 2015, the 0014Squad was a group with the interests of airsoft and tactical games. After one of it's founders leaving, the 0014Squad was repurposed as a BLOCKADE 3D clan in 2016 and a multi-gaming community with an Unturned server in 2017.

In December 2018 the 0014Squad officially disbanded after pilling up problems and the members left merged with the German clan TakeThat for 4 months and later became known as the Yusi0014.

Today the 0014Squad only exists for nostalgia and a hub for the original members and friends.
